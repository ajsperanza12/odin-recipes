Pizza

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes of all time. Passed down by my great ancestors, this is a staple to all human cuisine. I couldn't live without it.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Mom's Sauce

Steps

  1. Lay out dough in circle
  2. Sauce up the dough
  3. Add cheese
  4. Cook in oven
  5. Enjoy
